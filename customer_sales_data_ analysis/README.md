Customer Sales Analysis Project
📌 Objective

Analyze customer purchasing behavior to identify top customers, revenue distribution, and product popularity.

🛠 Tools & Technologies

Python

Pandas

Matplotlib

Seaborn

📊 Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following columns:

customer_id → Unique ID of customer

name → Customer name

city → Customer location

product → Purchased product

amount → Purchase amount

📈 Analysis Performed
1. Total Spending per Customer

Calculated total amount spent by each customer

2. Top Customer

Identified the highest spending customer

3. Revenue by City

Analyzed which city generates the most revenue

4. Product Popularity

Counted frequency of products purchased

5. Average Spending

Calculated average spending per customer

📊 Visualizations

Bar chart → Total spending per customer

Bar chart → Revenue by city

Count plot → Product popularity

🔍 Key Insights

Priya is the top customer with highest spending

Delhi generates the highest revenue

Laptop is the most popular product

Customer spending varies significantly across individuals

🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates:

Data cleaning and transformation

Data aggregation using pandas

Visualization using matplotlib and seaborn

Business insight extraction
